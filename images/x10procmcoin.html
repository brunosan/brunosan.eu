Product Description

X10 Pro CM11A Computer Interface

CM11A: Computer Interface , cable  and software. The interface  connects directly to the serial port of a home PC to enable it to control any X10 automation  system. It is not essential  that the PC remain turned  "ON".  All information and settings are downloadable to the Interface.

The CM11A is also the choice of numerous OEM Automation Software manufactures given its ease to which it can interface between the PC and AC powerline
