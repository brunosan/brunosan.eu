APOD

On May,22 2007 I had the pleasure to publish on the NASA Astronomy Picture of the Day.

Thank you to the AIG to host the video files. It has been served thousands of times already... The whole server went even down the day of the publication!

I have also published some proceedings about the first months of visits.

UPDATE:

On April,5 2009, to announce the SunDay inside de 100 Hours of Astronomy, the editors decided to show that same APOD again. Since I am one of the responsibles for SunDay and I also help with 100 hours, I guess it makes some sense. Anyway, it was a big nice surprise!

Thank you to the AIG to still host the video files. It has been served thousands of times and counting… The whole server went even down again the day of the re-publication!
