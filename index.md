[Young Global Leader](/2013/03/12/young-global-leader-2013/) of the World Economic Forum. [PhD](/2008/11/13/phd/) Magna Cum Laude in Astrophysics by the University of Goeetingen and the Max Planck for Solar System Research. [Postdoctoral Fellow at NRL](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Naval_Research_Laboratory) on NASA satellites and rocket science. [Mirzayan](/2010/12/09/101-days-as-science-and-technology-policy-fellow/) Fellow of the US National Academies of Sciences. Former Director for Science and Technology at the NGO [Gain](http://gain.org/) on climate change Adaptiation. Former Chief Scientist for the mapping company [Mapbox](www.mapbox.com).
